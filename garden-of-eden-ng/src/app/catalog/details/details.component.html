Pelargonium peltatum

  • Light: loves full sun exposure
  • Water: regularly, but in small quantity don't soil the root too much, unless root rot
  • Fertilizer: once every two weeks during bloom and once a month winter time
  • Temperature: 15 - 30 Degrees Celsius
  • Humidity: at least 40%, if wheather is hot must be sprayed on with humidifier
